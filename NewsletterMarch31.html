Important Announcement: Resolution of Litigation Regarding Broker Commissions

I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to personally reach out to share some significant news in the real estate industry that may affect you and your investments.

Recently, there has been a groundbreaking development regarding the resolution of litigation surrounding broker commissions by the National Association of REALTORSÂ® (NAR).

Settlement Overview:
On March 15, 2024, NAR announced an agreement aimed at resolving litigation concerning claims brought on behalf of home sellers regarding broker commissions. Pending court approval, NAR has agreed to pay $418 million over approximately four years. It's important to note that NAR maintains its stance of denying any wrongdoing in connection with the Multiple Listing Service (MLS) cooperative compensation model rule introduced in the 1990s.

Key Achievements:
This resolution brings about several significant achievements, including:

Release from Liability: The agreement releases most NAR members and numerous industry stakeholders from liability in these matters, providing clarity and protection moving forward.

Preservation of Consumer Choice: Cooperative compensation remains a choice for consumers when buying or selling a home, ensuring continued flexibility and accessibility within the real estate market.

New MLS Rule: NAR has agreed to implement a new MLS rule prohibiting offers of broker compensation on the MLS. While offers of compensation will not be communicated via the MLS, consumers can still pursue them off-MLS through negotiation with real estate professionals.

Buyer Representation: A new rule will require MLS participants working with buyers to enter into written agreements with their buyers, enhancing transparency and clarity in buyer representation.

Looking Ahead:
These changes are set to take effect in mid-July 2024, signifying a significant shift in industry practices. While adjustments may be necessary, NAR remains committed to leading the real estate industry forward and providing unmatched value to its members and American consumers.

As your trusted advisor in real estate investment, I will continue to monitor developments closely and provide guidance to ensure a seamless transition. If you have any questions or need further information, please don't hesitate to reach out.
