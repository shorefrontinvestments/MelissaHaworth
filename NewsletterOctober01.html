Maximizing Profits: The Sub 2 Approach

~~ 😀 🌴 🏡 ~~

Hi there,

 

"Sub 2" is a real estate investment strategy where a property is acquired under the existing mortgage. The buyer assumes ownership, but the seller's loan remains in their name, with the buyer taking on the responsibility of making mortgage payments.

In a Sub 2 deal, the buyer enjoys several advantages:

- No need to qualify for a new loan
- No requirement for a down payment
- No necessity for a high credit score
- Control over the property
- Assumption of the existing financing
- Payment of the existing mortgage on behalf of the seller
- Management of the mortgage payments on the seller's existing loan

This approach provides flexibility and accessibility for buyers, allowing them to navigate real estate transactions without the traditional hurdles associated with securing a new mortgage.

Sub 2 deals are also beneficial for sellers because they:

- Can quickly sell a property
- Are not responsible for closing costs
- Avoid foreclosure
- Have the opportunity to rebuild credit with consistent payments

These advantages make Sub 2 an attractive option for both buyers and sellers in the real estate market, providing a mutually beneficial solution that addresses various financial considerations.
